About Me

I am a graduating BS Computer Science student at Universidad de Dagupan with experience in project management, system development, records administration, student leadership, and creative production.

My work connects technology and communication, from developing transportation and voting systems to managing enterprise software projects and directing award-winning tourism and advocacy videos.

I enjoy leading teams, organizing requirements, writing clear documentation, and building systems that solve real problems for students, commuters, schools, and communities.

I am open to entry-level roles, and collaborations where I can contribute as a developer, project coordinator, technical documenter, or emerging technology leader.
